Research Interest
I am broadly interest in AI Surveillance, Machine Learning, Data Science. My currently research focuses on Person Re-identification, Deep Fake Detection and AI Model Cross Domain Adaptation, Generalization, Optimisation and Deployment. I am passionate for developing feasible solutions for real-world problems using Artificial Intelligence.
