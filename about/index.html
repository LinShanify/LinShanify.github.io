About Me

Nov 28 2019 2 minutes read (About 276 words)

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, University of Warwick. I work under the supervision of Prof. Chang-Tsun Li.

Research Interest

My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and transfer learning, and particularly person matching and recognition in video surveillance system (Person Re-Identification).

Research Experience

  • Research Assistant | Hong Kong Baptist University | Dec 2016 - Mar 2017
