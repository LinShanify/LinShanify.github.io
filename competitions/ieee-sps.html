System Overview

Our entry was entitled "Visual Analytics System for Pandemic Management during COVID-19". It utilizes AI-based video analytics for contact tracing and pandemic control functions. By using various AI models developed by the ROSE Lab, the proposed platform performs quick person searches and contact tracking within a network of surveillance cameras, while proactively monitoring for safety violations.

Person Detection
Real-time AI-based pedestrian detection across the camera network
Face Mask Detection
Automated detection of individuals not wearing face masks
Social Distance Monitoring
Alarm system triggered when social distancing violations are detected
Person Re-identification
Contact tracing through cross-camera person matching
Path Tracing
Historical trajectory reconstruction for contact tracing investigations
Crowd Heat Map
Real-time population density visualization for crowd management
